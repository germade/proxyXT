# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 4, 2026_

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **ProxyXT**. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information ProxyXT accesses, how it is used, and the choices you have.

## 2. Information We Collect

ProxyXT does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

All data created while using the extension — including proxy server configurations (host, port, scheme, bypass rules, and optional aliases) — is stored exclusively on your device using the browser's built-in local storage (`chrome.storage.local` / `browser.storage.local`). This data never leaves your device.

ProxyXT does **not**:

- Create or require user accounts.
- Collect browsing history or traffic data.
- Send any data to external servers or third parties.
- Use analytics, telemetry, or crash-reporting services.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The proxy configurations you save are used solely to apply proxy settings to your browser via the browser's native proxy API. No data is processed outside your device.

Internal debug logs generated by the extension are stored temporarily in local storage and are only accessible to you through the extension's built-in log viewer. They are never transmitted anywhere.

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. You can remove it at any time by:

- Deleting individual proxy servers through the extension interface.
- Uninstalling the extension, which removes all associated storage.

## 5. Third-Party Services

ProxyXT does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs.

The proxy servers you configure are external services of your own choosing. ProxyXT is not responsible for the privacy practices of those servers.

## 6. Permissions

ProxyXT requests the following browser permissions:

| Permission | Reason |
|---|---|
| `proxy` | Required to apply and clear proxy settings in the browser. |
| `storage` | Required to save and load your proxy configurations locally. |

No other permissions are requested.

## 7. Children's Privacy

ProxyXT does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published in the extension's repository. Continued use of the extension after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

## 9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in the extension's repository.
